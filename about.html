About

I'm a self-taught intuitive tarot reader and web developer. I always had a vision of merging tech with my spiritual work. The deeper I went into my "woo woo" endeavors, the more I realized I need an online presence in order to serve others. At first, I tried building a personal website through cookie-cutter website builder platforms, but never felt satisfied because of their limitations.This inspired me to learn how to build websites from scratch, not only to launch my own site, but to also help spiritual entrepreneurs who have no technical background and wish to put themselves out there.


I understand the struggle of being an intuitive reader, energy healer, life coach, astrologer, etc. and having SO much to offer, yet feeling overwhelmed with how to build an online presence. As I started dabbling with code, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my spiritual work. Now I can use my creativity and eye for detail to build any kind of website imagineable for myself and others.

Mission

This sacred space is meant to empower those on the non-linear path to individuation. While Tarot is the main focus, you will also explore a variety of topics such as Astrology, Jungian Psychology, Creative Visualization, Meditation, Intuitive Development, Dreamwork, Hermeticism, Eastern Philosophy, Holistic Healing, Crystals, Ritual, Grief Work, and much more. My goal is not to dogmatize, but to offer loving support and share knowledge. No matter where you are on your spiritual journey, beginner or veteran, there is something for everyone here. Stick with whatever resonates.


Esotericism has been an undying passion of mine since I can remember. I've always sensed that there's something larger than life, a unifying Higher intelligence. Having experienced extreme loss in my childhood thinned the veil to Spirit and expanded my perspective on life, death, and the Universe. I envision myself helping others connect to their own Divine truth.
