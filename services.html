What I Do

Intuitive Tarot Readings


Are you trying to understand the energy dynamics within a relationship? Do you need clarity on how to overcome adversity? Do you wish to change your story, but don’t know where to begin? Intuitive Readings help identify and release negative patterns and outdated beliefs that are limiting you from your highest potential. I receive information mainly through clairsentience (clear feeling) and claircognizance (clear knowing). By tuning into your energy field and connecting with your Spirit Guides, I use Tarot to convey dominant themes and lessons that are currently at play in your life.

FAQ

  • How will I receive my Intuitive Tarot Reading?

    You will receive a PDF document containing an extensive written interpretation of your Intuitive Reading along with a picture of your unique Tarot spread (via email within 72 hours of payment received). Live readings are not offered at this time.

  • Do we need to meet in person for the reading to be more accurate?

    Rest assured that time and distance do not affect a reading's accuracy.

  • Do you use specific Tarot spreads when giving readings?

    I intuitively pull as many cards I feel are necessary, depending on the complexity of the reading.

  • What can I expect from an Intuive Tarot Reading?

    Results vary for everyone, but overall, Intuitive Tarot Readings are uplifting and provide clarity, heal old wounds, bring higher awareness on unhealthy triggers and behavioral tendencies in order to shift them, and help you better understand your Soul's purpose in this lifetime.

  • How can I get the most out of an Intuitive Tarot Reading?

    Set positive intentions beforehand. Release any expectations and be open to what IS.

  • How can I formulate a clear question for an Intuitive Tarot Reading?

    Ask questions that begin with How/What/Why and avoid asking questions that begin with Should/Will/When.

  • Do you predict the future?

    This has always been a common misconception about intuitive work. Intuitive readings of any kind are meant to offer a Higher perspective that is channeled from Divine Spirit helpers and Higher Self. While it is possible to tune into a possible outcome based on current life path energies, nothing is ever set in stone because of free will.

  • Are Intuitive Tarot Readings safe?

    Yes, very safe! There's no reason to fear this kind of work, as it's aligned to Divine Source/All That Is.

  • Can you communicate with Spirits that have crossed over?

    I do not offer Mediumship readings at this time.

  • Where can I find additional information on your services?

    Please contact me for pricing information and all other inquiries.
